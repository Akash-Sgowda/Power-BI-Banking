# Power-BI-Banking

# Overview
The Bank Analytics Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project designed to provide actionable insights into banking operations, customer behavior, and loan performance.
It enables stakeholders to monitor key financial metrics, analyze loan trends, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

# Objectives
Analyze overall loan distribution and growth over time.
Track interest rates, revolving balances, and received amounts.
Understand customer segmentation based on verification status, home ownership, and geography.
Identify high-value loan purposes and sub-grade performance.

# Dashboard Insights
1. Loan Trend Analysis
   Year-wise loan growth from 2007 to 2011.
   Significant increase in loan amounts, indicating rising customer demand.

2. Payment Analysis
   Comparison of Verified, Not Verified, and Source Verified payments.
   Helps assess risk and trust levels in loan approvals.

3. Customer Segmentation
   Loan distribution by Home Ownership (Mortgage, Rent, Own, Other).
   Sub-grade-wise analysis of Revolving Balances.

4. Geographic Analysis
   State-wise loan status visualization using an interactive map.
   Identifies regional loan concentration and performance.

5. Loan Purpose Analysis
   Loans are primarily used for debt consolidation and credit card repayment, indicating a strong focus on debt management.
   Home improvement and small business loans form a significant secondary segment, reflecting investment-driven borrowing.
   Personal and discretionary purposes such as medical, education, vacation, and renewable energy contribute comparatively less.

# Key Learnings
Designed business-focused KPIs for financial analysis. 
Improved skills in data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization best practices. 
Gained hands-on experience in banking and financial domain analytics.
